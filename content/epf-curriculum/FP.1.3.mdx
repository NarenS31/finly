# Module FP.1.3: Philanthropy & Community

## Standard: EPF.FP.1.3

**Objective:** Explain the role of philanthropy and community involvement in financial planning.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define philanthropy and community giving
- Discuss benefits to individuals and society
- Analyze ways to give (time, money, skills)

## Teacher Notes
- Use local charity examples
- Relate to students' volunteer experiences
- Discuss tax benefits of giving

## Activities
- Volunteer project planning
- Guest speaker: Nonprofit leader
- Create a personal giving plan

## Assessment
- Quiz on philanthropy concepts
- Written reflection on giving
