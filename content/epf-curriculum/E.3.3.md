# Module E.3.3: Taxes & Government Services

## Standard: EPF.E.3.3

**Objective:** Explain how taxes fund government services and affect the economy.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define types of taxes (income, sales, property)
- Discuss how tax revenue is used
- Analyze the impact of taxes on individuals and businesses

## Teacher Notes
- Use local/state tax data
- Discuss government budgets and spending
- Relate to student experiences (sales tax, school funding)

## Activities
- Budget simulation: Allocating tax revenue
- Debate: Are taxes fair?
- Research: Where do your taxes go?

## Assessment
- Quiz on tax types and uses
- Group budget presentation
