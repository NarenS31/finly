# Module CC.2.3: Consumer Self-Protection

## Standard: EPF.CC.2.3

**Objective:** Explain strategies for consumer self-protection and advocacy.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Discuss self-protection strategies (warranties, contracts, receipts)
- Analyze how to resolve disputes
- Explore consumer advocacy organizations

## Teacher Notes
- Use real dispute scenarios
- Relate to students' purchases
- Discuss how to escalate complaints

## Activities
- Dispute resolution role-play
- Create a consumer self-protection checklist
- Guest speaker: Consumer advocate

## Assessment
- Quiz on self-protection strategies
- Written plan for resolving a dispute
