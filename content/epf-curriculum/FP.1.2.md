# Module FP.1.2: Factors in Financial Planning

## Standard: EPF.FP.1.2

**Objective:** Identify factors that influence personal financial planning.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Discuss factors (age, income, goals, risk tolerance)
- Analyze how these affect financial decisions
- Create a personal financial plan

## Teacher Notes
- Use student scenarios
- Relate to students' current/future plans
- Discuss short- vs. long-term goals

## Activities
- Financial planning worksheet
- Group discussion: Setting goals
- Guest speaker: Financial planner

## Assessment
- Completed financial plan
- Quiz on planning factors
