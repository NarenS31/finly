# Module CC.1.1: Advertising & Consumer Decisions

## Standard: EPF.CC.1.1

**Objective:** Analyze the impact of advertising on consumer decisions.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define advertising and marketing
- Discuss persuasive techniques
- Analyze effects on spending habits

## Teacher Notes
- Use real ads and commercials
- Relate to students' purchases
- Discuss social media influence

## Activities
- Ad analysis project
- Create a persuasive ad
- Debate: Are ads ethical?

## Assessment
- Quiz on advertising techniques
- Written analysis of an ad
