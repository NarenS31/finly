# Module MCM.1.2: Critiquing Plans

## Standard: EPF.MCM.1.2

**Objective:** Critique and revise personal spending plans for improvement.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Review sample budgets
- Identify strengths and weaknesses
- Suggest improvements

## Teacher Notes
- Use peer review of budgets
- Discuss common budgeting mistakes
- Relate to students' own plans

## Activities
- Budget critique workshop
- Group discussion: Budgeting pitfalls
- Revise a sample budget

## Assessment
- Written critique of a budget
- Participation in workshop
