# Module E.1.2: Market Structures

## Standard: EPF.E.1.2

**Objective:** Compare characteristics of different market structures (perfect competition, monopoly, oligopoly, monopolistic competition).

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define each market structure
- Identify real-world examples
- Analyze advantages and disadvantages

## Teacher Notes
- Use diagrams to illustrate market structures
- Discuss current events involving monopolies/oligopolies
- Facilitate group discussions on market power

## Activities
- Market structure simulation game
- Case study: Famous monopolies
- Chart: Compare features of each structure

## Assessment
- Quiz on definitions and examples
- Group presentation on assigned structure

## Questions

1. **Multiple Choice:** Which market structure has the most competition?
   - a) Monopoly
   - b) Oligopoly
   - c) Perfect competition
   - d) Monopolistic competition

2. **Short Answer:** Give an example of a real-world monopoly.

3. **Multiple Choice:** In which market structure do firms have the most control over price?
   - a) Perfect competition
   - b) Monopoly
   - c) Oligopoly
   - d) Monopolistic competition

4. **Short Answer:** List one advantage and one disadvantage of an oligopoly.

5. **Multiple Choice:** Which structure is most likely to use advertising to differentiate products?
   - a) Perfect competition
   - b) Monopoly
   - c) Monopolistic competition
   - d) Oligopoly

6. **Short Answer:** Explain why barriers to entry are important in a monopoly.

7. **Multiple Choice:** Which of the following is NOT a characteristic of perfect competition?
   - a) Many buyers and sellers
   - b) Identical products
   - c) Difficult entry
   - d) Easy exit

8. **Short Answer:** How can government regulation affect market structures?
