# Module MCM.1.3: Renting, Leasing, Owning

## Standard: EPF.MCM.1.3

**Objective:** Compare the costs and benefits of renting, leasing, and owning property.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define renting, leasing, owning
- Analyze pros and cons of each
- Discuss long-term financial impacts

## Teacher Notes
- Use real estate listings and calculators
- Relate to students' future housing choices
- Discuss local market conditions

## Activities
- Housing cost comparison project
- Guest speaker: Real estate agent
- Debate: Rent vs. own

## Assessment
- Written analysis of housing options
- Quiz on key terms
