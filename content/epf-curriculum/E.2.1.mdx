# Module E.2.1: Macroeconomic Indicators

## Standard: EPF.E.2.1

**Objective:** Describe key macroeconomic indicators (GDP, unemployment, inflation) and their significance.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define GDP, unemployment, inflation
- Explain how each is measured
- Discuss why these indicators matter

## Teacher Notes
- Use current data from government sources
- Show graphs and trends over time
- Relate to students' lives (jobs, prices)

## Activities
- Data analysis: Track indicators over time
- News article review
- Create an "economic health" report card

## Assessment
- Quiz on definitions and significance
- Group presentation on one indicator
