# Module CC.2.1: Consumer Protection Laws

## Standard: EPF.CC.2.1

**Objective:** Describe major consumer protection laws and agencies.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify key consumer protection laws
- Discuss the role of agencies (FTC, CFPB, etc.)
- Analyze how laws protect consumers

## Teacher Notes
- Use real case studies
- Relate to students' experiences with products/services
- Discuss how to file a complaint

## Activities
- Law/agency matching game
- Research a consumer protection case
- Guest speaker: Consumer protection agency

## Assessment
- Quiz on laws and agencies
- Written summary of a case
