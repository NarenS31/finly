# Module MCM.2.2: Interest & Fees

## Standard: EPF.MCM.2.2

**Objective:** Explain how interest and fees affect financial decisions.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define interest (simple, compound) and fees
- Analyze how they impact savings and loans
- Discuss strategies to minimize fees

## Teacher Notes
- Use real account statements
- Relate to students' savings/checking accounts
- Discuss credit card interest

## Activities
- Interest calculation activity
- Fee comparison worksheet
- Guest speaker: Financial advisor

## Assessment
- Quiz on interest and fees
- Written plan to minimize fees
