# Module CC.1.2: Information for Decisions

## Standard: EPF.CC.1.2

**Objective:** Evaluate sources of information for making consumer decisions.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify reliable vs. unreliable sources
- Discuss research strategies
- Analyze the impact of misinformation

## Teacher Notes
- Use product review sites
- Relate to students' online shopping
- Discuss confirmation bias

## Activities
- Source reliability comparison
- Research a product purchase
- Guest speaker: Consumer advocate

## Assessment
- Quiz on information sources
- Written product review
