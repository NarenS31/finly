# Module E.2.4: Economic Organizations

## Standard: EPF.E.2.4

**Objective:** Describe the role of economic organizations (IMF, World Bank, WTO, etc.) in the global economy.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify major economic organizations
- Explain their purposes and functions
- Discuss their impact on global trade and development

## Teacher Notes
- Use case studies of organization interventions
- Discuss controversies and criticisms
- Relate to current global events

## Activities
- Research project: Organization profiles
- Model UN simulation
- News analysis: Organization in action

## Assessment
- Presentation on assigned organization
- Quiz on organization roles
