# Module MCM.2.3: Payment Methods

## Standard: EPF.MCM.2.3

**Objective:** Compare different payment methods and their advantages/disadvantages.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify payment methods (cash, check, debit, credit, digital)
- Discuss security and convenience factors
- Analyze costs and risks

## Teacher Notes
- Use real payment tools (cards, apps)
- Relate to students' experiences
- Discuss fraud prevention

## Activities
- Payment method comparison chart
- Scenario analysis: Choosing the best method
- Guest speaker: Fraud prevention expert

## Assessment
- Quiz on payment methods
- Written analysis of a payment scenario
