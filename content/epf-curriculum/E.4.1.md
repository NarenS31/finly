# Module E.4.1: Trade & Interdependence

## Standard: EPF.E.4.1

**Objective:** Explain the benefits and costs of trade and economic interdependence.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define trade, imports, exports, interdependence
- Discuss comparative and absolute advantage
- Analyze effects of trade on economies

## Teacher Notes
- Use real-world trade data
- Discuss current trade agreements and disputes
- Relate to local/global products

## Activities
- Trade simulation game
- Research: Where do products come from?
- Debate: Is free trade good?

## Assessment
- Quiz on trade concepts
- Written analysis of a trade issue
