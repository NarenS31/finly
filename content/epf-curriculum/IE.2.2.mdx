# Module IE.2.2: Types & Purposes of Taxes

## Standard: EPF.IE.2.2

**Objective:** Describe different types of taxes and their purposes.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define income, sales, property, and other taxes
- Discuss why taxes are collected
- Analyze who pays which taxes

## Teacher Notes
- Use local/state tax examples
- Relate to students' purchases and family taxes
- Discuss tax fairness and policy

## Activities
- Tax type matching game
- Research: Where do your taxes go?
- Debate: Are taxes fair?

## Assessment
- Quiz on tax types and purposes
- Written argument on tax fairness
