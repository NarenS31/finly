# Module E.2.2: Microeconomic Indicators

## Standard: EPF.E.2.2

**Objective:** Identify and explain key microeconomic indicators (business profits, consumer spending, etc.).

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define microeconomic indicators
- Discuss how businesses and consumers are measured
- Analyze trends and their impact

## Teacher Notes
- Use local business examples
- Relate to student spending habits
- Compare micro vs. macro indicators

## Activities
- Survey: Track class spending
- Case study: Local business profits
- Chart micro vs. macro indicators

## Assessment
- Quiz on micro indicators
- Written analysis of a business trend
