# Module E.3.2: Regulation

## Standard: EPF.E.3.2

**Objective:** Analyze the role of government regulation in the economy.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define regulation and deregulation
- Discuss reasons for regulation
- Explore examples in different industries

## Teacher Notes
- Use current regulatory news
- Discuss pros and cons of regulation
- Relate to student experiences (school rules, safety)

## Activities
- Regulatory impact simulation
- Debate: Should government regulate X?
- Analyze regulatory case studies

## Assessment
- Quiz on regulation concepts
- Written argument for/against regulation
