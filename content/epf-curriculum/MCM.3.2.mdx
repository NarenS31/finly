# Module MCM.3.2: Debt Management

## Standard: EPF.MCM.3.2

**Objective:** Explain strategies for managing and reducing debt.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify types of debt (good vs. bad)
- Discuss repayment strategies
- Analyze consequences of excessive debt

## Teacher Notes
- Use debt payoff calculators
- Relate to student/parent experiences
- Discuss bankruptcy and credit counseling

## Activities
- Debt repayment simulation
- Guest speaker: Debt management expert
- Create a debt reduction plan

## Assessment
- Quiz on debt management
- Written debt reduction plan
