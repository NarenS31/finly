# Module IE.1.1: Education, Income, Career, Lifestyle

## Standard: EPF.IE.1.1

**Objective:** Explain how education, income, career, and lifestyle choices impact personal financial goals.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Explore the relationship between education and earning potential
- Discuss career pathways and lifestyle choices
- Connect personal values to financial goals

## Teacher Notes
- Use real salary data and career statistics
- Invite guest speakers or use video interviews
- Encourage students to set personal financial goals

## Activities
- Career interest survey
- Budget simulation based on different careers
- Reflection: "How do my choices affect my future?"

## Assessment
- Written reflection
- Participation in simulation
