# Module MCM.1.4: Mortgages

## Standard: EPF.MCM.1.4

**Objective:** Explain the process and costs of obtaining a mortgage.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define mortgage and related terms
- Discuss steps in buying a home
- Analyze costs (interest, down payment, insurance)

## Teacher Notes
- Use mortgage calculators
- Relate to local housing market
- Discuss credit and loan approval

## Activities
- Mortgage calculation activity
- Guest speaker: Loan officer
- Create a home-buying checklist

## Assessment
- Completed mortgage worksheet
- Quiz on mortgage concepts
