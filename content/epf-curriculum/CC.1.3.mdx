# Module CC.1.3: Consumer Impact on Economy

## Standard: EPF.CC.1.3

**Objective:** Explain how consumer choices impact the economy.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Discuss the role of consumers in the economy
- Analyze the effects of spending and saving
- Explore ethical and environmental impacts

## Teacher Notes
- Use real-world examples (boycotts, trends)
- Relate to students' spending habits
- Discuss sustainability

## Activities
- Case study: Consumer movement
- Create a "spending impact" chart
- Debate: Should consumers buy local?

## Assessment
- Quiz on consumer impact
- Written reflection on spending choices
