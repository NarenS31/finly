# Module IE.1.3: Postsecondary Costs & Income

## Standard: EPF.IE.1.3

**Objective:** Analyze the costs and benefits of postsecondary education and its impact on income.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify types of postsecondary education
- Calculate costs (tuition, fees, living expenses)
- Compare expected incomes by education level

## Teacher Notes
- Use real college cost data
- Discuss scholarships, grants, loans
- Relate to students' future plans

## Activities
- College cost calculator activity
- Research: ROI of different degrees
- Financial aid workshop

## Assessment
- Budget for postsecondary path
- Quiz on costs and benefits
