# Module IE.1.2: Career/Education Options

## Standard: EPF.IE.1.2

**Objective:** Evaluate career and education options and their impact on financial goals.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Explore different career paths
- Compare education requirements and costs
- Analyze long-term financial outcomes

## Teacher Notes
- Use career planning tools
- Discuss trade schools, college, apprenticeships
- Relate to students' interests and goals

## Activities
- Career research project
- Education cost comparison
- Guest speaker: Career panel

## Assessment
- Career plan presentation
- Quiz on education options
