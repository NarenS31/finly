# Module IE.2.1: Payroll Deductions

## Standard: EPF.IE.2.1

**Objective:** Explain common payroll deductions and their purposes.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify types of payroll deductions (taxes, insurance, retirement)
- Analyze a sample pay stub
- Discuss the impact on take-home pay

## Teacher Notes
- Use real or sample pay stubs
- Relate to students' part-time jobs
- Discuss voluntary vs. mandatory deductions

## Activities
- Pay stub analysis activity
- Create a deduction glossary
- Guest speaker: HR/payroll professional

## Assessment
- Quiz on deduction types
- Written explanation of a pay stub
