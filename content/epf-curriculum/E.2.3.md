# Module E.2.3: Fiscal & Monetary Policy

## Standard: EPF.E.2.3

**Objective:** Explain the roles of fiscal and monetary policy in the economy.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define fiscal and monetary policy
- Identify key institutions (Congress, Federal Reserve)
- Discuss policy tools and effects

## Teacher Notes
- Use recent policy examples (stimulus, interest rates)
- Compare/contrast fiscal vs. monetary
- Discuss policy impacts on students' lives

## Activities
- Policy simulation game
- Debate: Which policy is more effective?
- Analyze news headlines

## Assessment
- Quiz on policy definitions
- Group policy proposal
