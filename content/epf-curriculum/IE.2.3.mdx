# Module IE.2.3: Tax Form Preparation

## Standard: EPF.IE.2.3

**Objective:** Demonstrate how to prepare basic tax forms (W-4, 1040, etc.).

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify common tax forms
- Walk through completing a sample 1040
- Discuss importance of accurate tax filing

## Teacher Notes
- Use IRS sample forms
- Relate to students' first jobs
- Discuss consequences of errors

## Activities
- Complete a sample 1040
- Tax scenario role-play
- Guest speaker: Tax preparer

## Assessment
- Completed sample tax form
- Quiz on tax form parts
