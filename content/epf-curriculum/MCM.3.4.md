# Module MCM.3.4: Insurance & Estate Planning

## Standard: EPF.MCM.3.4

**Objective:** Explain the purpose and types of insurance and basics of estate planning.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define insurance types (health, auto, life, property)
- Discuss why insurance is important
- Introduce estate planning basics

## Teacher Notes
- Use real insurance policy examples
- Relate to students' family experiences
- Discuss wills, beneficiaries, and power of attorney

## Activities
- Insurance needs analysis
- Guest speaker: Insurance agent
- Create a simple estate plan

## Assessment
- Quiz on insurance types
- Written estate plan outline
