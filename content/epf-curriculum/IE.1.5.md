# Module IE.1.5: Types of Income

## Standard: EPF.IE.1.5

**Objective:** Distinguish between different types of income (earned, unearned, passive).

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define earned, unearned, and passive income
- Provide examples of each type
- Discuss tax implications

## Teacher Notes
- Use real pay stubs and investment examples
- Relate to students' family income sources
- Discuss side hustles and gig economy

## Activities
- Income source scavenger hunt
- Create a personal income chart
- Tax scenario analysis

## Assessment
- Quiz on income types
- Written reflection on income sources
