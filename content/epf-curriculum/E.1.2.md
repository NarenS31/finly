# Module E.1.2: Market Structures

## Standard: EPF.E.1.2

**Objective:** Compare characteristics of different market structures (perfect competition, monopoly, oligopoly, monopolistic competition).

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define each market structure
- Identify real-world examples
- Analyze advantages and disadvantages

## Teacher Notes
- Use diagrams to illustrate market structures
- Discuss current events involving monopolies/oligopolies
- Facilitate group discussions on market power

## Activities
- Market structure simulation game
- Case study: Famous monopolies
- Chart: Compare features of each structure

## Assessment
- Quiz on definitions and examples
- Group presentation on assigned structure
