# Module FP.1.1: Investing Strategies

## Standard: EPF.FP.1.1

**Objective:** Explain basic investing strategies and their risks/rewards.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define investing and common strategies
- Discuss risk vs. reward
- Analyze types of investments (stocks, bonds, mutual funds)

## Teacher Notes
- Use real investment data
- Relate to students' future goals
- Discuss diversification

## Activities
- Investment simulation game
- Guest speaker: Financial advisor
- Create a sample investment portfolio

## Assessment
- Quiz on investment types
- Written investment plan
