# Module E.3.1: Legal Structure & Property Rights

## Standard: EPF.E.3.1

**Objective:** Explain the importance of legal structure and property rights in the economy.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define legal structure and property rights
- Discuss their role in economic growth
- Analyze examples of weak vs. strong property rights

## Teacher Notes
- Use historical and global comparisons
- Relate to local property issues
- Discuss government role in enforcement

## Activities
- Case study: Countries with different property rights
- Debate: Eminent domain
- Research local property laws

## Assessment
- Quiz on definitions and examples
- Group presentation on property rights
