# Module MCM.1.1: Creating a Spending Plan

## Standard: EPF.MCM.1.1

**Objective:** Develop a personal spending plan (budget) based on financial goals.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define budgeting and its importance
- Identify income and expenses
- Create a sample budget

## Teacher Notes
- Use real-life scenarios
- Relate to students' current/future finances
- Discuss needs vs. wants

## Activities
- Budget simulation activity
- Needs vs. wants sorting game
- Reflection: Budgeting challenges

## Assessment
- Completed budget worksheet
- Quiz on budgeting concepts
