## Start with this question

Before spending money, ask: "Do I need this right now, or do I just want it?"

A **need** helps you live safely and learn well.
A **want** is nice to have, but you can live without it.

## Why sorting matters

When you sort spending into needs and wants, you build a pause button in your brain. That pause is what helps you avoid
impulse buys you regret, and it also helps you notice when a “want” is actually important for your wellbeing — like a
sport that keeps you active, which can sit between categories depending on your family rules.
