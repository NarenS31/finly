# Module MCM.2.1: Using Financial Services

## Standard: EPF.MCM.2.1

**Objective:** Evaluate types of financial services and their uses.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify types of financial institutions
- Compare services (checking, savings, loans)
- Discuss how to choose a financial institution

## Teacher Notes
- Use local bank/credit union examples
- Relate to students' experiences
- Discuss online vs. traditional banking

## Activities
- Bank comparison project
- Guest speaker: Bank representative
- Create a checklist for choosing a bank

## Assessment
- Written comparison of services
- Quiz on financial institutions
