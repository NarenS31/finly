# Module CC.2.2: Fraudulent Practices

## Standard: EPF.CC.2.2

**Objective:** Identify common fraudulent and deceptive practices and how to avoid them.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define fraud and deception
- Discuss common scams (online, phone, in-person)
- Analyze prevention strategies

## Teacher Notes
- Use real scam examples
- Relate to students' online experiences
- Discuss reporting fraud

## Activities
- Scam scenario role-play
- Create a fraud prevention guide
- Guest speaker: Law enforcement

## Assessment
- Quiz on fraud types
- Written fraud prevention plan
