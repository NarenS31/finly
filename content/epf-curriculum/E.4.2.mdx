# Module E.4.2: NC in US/World Economy

## Standard: EPF.E.4.2

**Objective:** Analyze North Carolina's role in the US and global economy.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Identify NC's major industries and exports
- Discuss NC's economic connections
- Analyze the impact of global trends on NC

## Teacher Notes
- Use state economic data
- Invite local business speakers
- Relate to students' families and communities

## Activities
- Research project: NC's top exports
- Map NC's trade partners
- News analysis: Global events affecting NC

## Assessment
- Presentation on NC's economic role
- Quiz on NC industries
