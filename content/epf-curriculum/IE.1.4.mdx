# Module IE.1.4: Minimizing Education Costs

## Standard: EPF.IE.1.4

**Objective:** Identify strategies to minimize education costs and student debt.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Discuss scholarships, grants, and work-study
- Explore community college and transfer options
- Analyze student loan terms and repayment

## Teacher Notes
- Use real scholarship search tools
- Discuss FAFSA and financial aid process
- Relate to students' family experiences

## Activities
- Scholarship search challenge
- Compare loan offers
- Guest speaker: Financial aid officer

## Assessment
- Scholarship application draft
- Quiz on cost-saving strategies
