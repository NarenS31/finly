# Module MCM.3.1: Credit Sources

## Standard: EPF.MCM.3.1

**Objective:** Identify sources of credit and their uses.

---

## Lesson Overview
- Define credit and types (credit cards, loans, lines of credit)
- Discuss responsible credit use
- Analyze costs and benefits

## Teacher Notes
- Use real credit offers
- Relate to students' future borrowing
- Discuss credit scores and reports

## Activities
- Credit offer comparison
- Guest speaker: Credit counselor
- Create a credit decision flowchart

## Assessment
- Quiz on credit types
- Written plan for responsible credit use
